ELECTION RESULT MANAGEMENT SYSTEM (ERMS)

MONITOR INSTRUCTIONS (SMARTPHONE)

***YOU REQUIRE AN INTERNET ENABLED PHONE WITH AT LEAST 5 GB DATA)***

1.      Installation

 

Click on the link given to you and follow the instructions provided by your phone.

The phone will install the icon below on your phone which you will click on to use the app.

2.      Operations

2.1   Check In

When you arrive at your polling centre, click on the icon above to get to the check in screen.

        Enter your username and password

        On the monitor dashboard, view the information and click on Check In

        Click OK on successful check in

This will enable you and your supervisor to be able to communicate, and to capture the result of each election and submit the results to the UDF tally centre

2.2   Result Capture

If screen has timed out, login again

You will capture results of Presidential, Parliamentary and Local Government elections in that order.

        Click on Presidential

        Complete the form

        Attach photo of results (click attach image and take photo)

        Submit Result

Note:

1.      If prompted, review, and correct before confirming submission

2.      The above process should be done for the parliamentary result and local government result.

3.      Completion of this process will generate payment of allowance to monitor
